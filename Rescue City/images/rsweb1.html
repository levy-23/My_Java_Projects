Rescue Squad

Welcome to Rescue Squad! - A simulation of emergency vehicles responding to crimes being committed. There are two types of criminals in this world - Arsonists and Robbers. Arsonists set fire to random house and then admire their work. After doing so, they rush home before they are caught by the police. When a fire has been set, the fire trucks are immediately alerted, and rush to the scene to put out the fire with their water hose. Robbers on the other hand are the confrontational types. They rob houses in hope that the police will show up. When they do, they shoot the policemen and then rush home. Robbers have a maximum of five bullets and 3 bullets can kill one officer.
